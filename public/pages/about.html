My Inspirations

I've always had an admiration for intricate displays of art and aspired to be in a category of greatest creative orchestrators. I always wanted to create something so ahead of time, if not timeless, that will continue to flourish well beyond my time on this earth; a testimony of my legacy, if you will. I want people to look at my creation, flabbergasted in the level of imagination, grit and work ethic necessary to create something so revolutionary/iconic/jurassic. I want people to look at me the way the audience stared at the first iPhone, the first Tesla all electric automobile, the first artificial intelligent/machine learning chat bot. I want the world to look at me and my creations/accomplishments with unfathomable awe.

Part 2: Tech Lifestyle/Platform

Software engineering provides that platform and allows some of the most creative geniuses on earth to stand-out and stand tall next to their great(choose an other adjective with more dept) contribution towards a better world. I love finding solutions because of the numerous conclusions that keep me fully submerged in my work until the final product is complete and delivered to a more-than-satisfied client. And even then, theres always a better, more ways to to make a project better(say this part better). I love mob coding to collectively find innovative solutions. Like team meetings, great things come from geniuses alike but mob-coding is more focused on single solutions rather than the bigger picture, really allowing your team members to build (dynamic/genuine chemistry), learning and taking away each others strengths, and ultimately growing into a better team, organically.

Ever since I wrote mh first line of code, I immediately saw the potential! My drive for greatness has discovered an outlet that, picked up the practice, fell in love with the lifestyle and haven't looked back since. I was like a kid at magic show; my jaw dropped with fascination and wonder. After my first completed site, I knew this was something I wanted to do for the rest of my life.

In Conclusion my artistic admiration, infatuation with tech, joy in social practices and drive for greatness has catapulted/elevated(some dope finisher word) me into what I love and enjoy today, software engineering and development for a better world tomorrow.
